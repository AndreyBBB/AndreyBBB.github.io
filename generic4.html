Coffee sales (Excel)

  • It is a project guided by @mo-chen
  • I analyzed the coffee sales data and built an interactive dashboard
  • The initial data had about 1000 rows, was distributed between three sheets, and had a controversial date format
  • I populated the data into one sheet, changed data format for several columns, added new columns to use the full names instead of abbreviations
  • I made a dashboard, including charts, timeline and slicers to compare sales by country, by roast type, by loyalty program, and within any period of time
