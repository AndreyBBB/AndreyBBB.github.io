Restaurant delivery (Excel)

  • I analyzed the delivery data of the restaurant chain in California to figure out the duration of each delivery process step
  • The initial data had about 18000 rows, was disorganized, and had an unusable date format
  • I verified and cleaned the data, checked it for outliers and errors, transformed date columns, and added new columns, calculating the needed parameters
  • The final data was sorted by date in an easy-to-understand manner
  • I also made a summary pivot table presenting the key figures for every restaurant, helping the owners to understand where they should pay more attention
