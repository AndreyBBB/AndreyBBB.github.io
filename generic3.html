Survey dashboard (Power BI)

  • It is a project guided by @AlexTheAnalyst
  • I analyzed the results of a survey conducted among data specialists
  • I verified and cleaned the data in PowerBI, checked it for outliers and errors, created a new column with a new metric and regrouped several small categories of answers
  • I created an interactive dashboard to understand the differences between data specialists depending on their job titles, location, or gender
